Crafted with Care

HabitFlow isn't just a tracker, it's a space for you to acknowledge your efforts. In a busy world, taking a moment to check off a habit can be a small act of mindfulness.

This project combines modern web technologies with a design philosophy centered on user well-being. We utilize HTML5, Tailwind CSS, and jQuery to ensure a smooth, responsive experience.
